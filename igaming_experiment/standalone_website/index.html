Note on Methodology

Treatment Arms

Action / delivery scenario

  • Signs online casino compact.
  • Uses revenue to deliver each respondent's top priority.
  • Measures upside and risk of policy action plus delivery.

Inaction / no-delivery scenario

  • No top-priority delivery before the 2027 election.
  • No compact/revenue-delivery frame.
  • Measures downside of inaction and non-delivery.
